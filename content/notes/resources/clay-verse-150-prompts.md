# Clay Verse — 150 TikTok Video Prompts (Full Calendar)

## Production Specs
- Format: MP4, 9:16 vertical, 1080x1920
- Frame Rate: 24fps cinematic (claymation) or 30fps native
- Length: 15-60 seconds with trending sound integration
- Hashtag Strategy: 3-5 niche + 2-3 broad + 1 trending tag per video
- Engagement Hooks: Every video ends with a question or call-to-action
- Posting: 5 videos/day at 9 AM, 12 PM, 3 PM, 6 PM, 9 PM

## Material Specs
- Plasticine clay with visible fingerprints (1-3 per 10cm²)
- Matte finish (<10% reflectivity)
- Animation: 12fps stop-motion with 2-frame holds
- Clay deformation: 3-8% volume shift
- Camera: Physical slider movement with ±0.3 pixel jitter
- Color palette: Terracotta (#C65D07), ochre, sienna, sage green (30-70% saturation)

## Characters
- **Maya** — Investigator (main character, ordinary → magical hero)
- **The Veil** — Antagonist (tragic backstory, sacrificial redemption)
- **DJ** — Hacker
- **Alex** — Non-binary tech specialist
- **Captain Reeves** — Mentor
- **Luna** — Child prodigy
- **Best Friend** — Supporting throughout
- **Love Interest** — New neighbor (romance arc)
- **Pet** — Animal companion with magical awareness

## Workflow
Higgsfield.ai: Popcorn → Recast → Veo/Sora → Seedream for consistent character identity
